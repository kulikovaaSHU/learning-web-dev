About Someone's

Photography

I took my first photo when I was 5. It was on my mother's old Polaroid and the picture was the view of the rolling hills outside my window. I haven't stopped creating snap shots of my life since. I graduated from Unnamed Photography School and traveled the world. I enjoyed the quiet forests of Alaska and busy streets of Hong Kong. Watching life go by through my camera's viewfinder is the way I choose to live my life.
